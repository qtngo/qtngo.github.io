Pho-tastic Find in South Lake Tahoe!

Review Summary

Who would have thought that a weekend trip to South Lake Tahoe would yield the best hidden pho restaurants in all of Northern California. This weekend, we discovered a small family owned restaurant that just popped up 3 months now, "Lotus Pho". Their broth is amazingly seasoned, not to sweet, and not to impactful. The noodles fall apart, which leads us to believe they pre-blanched it. The ratio of meat to noodle was pretty good. Unfortunately, due to their location, fresh vegetables that usually accompanies a good bowl of pho was below average.

Grading Summary

Overall, Lotus Pho receives a solid 3.5/5 pho-icons! Keep up the pho-tastic work you guys!

Fla"pho"ful broth in Torrance

Review Summary

Sometimes the best places are the "hole-in-the-wall" that you randomly stopped by at. "Bamboo Song 2", located in Torrance/CA has an amazing Pho dish as well as other fusion styled foods, including Korean, Thai and Japanese dishes. For all of LAC, Bamboo Song 2 has the best broth that accompanies the entire dish. It doesn't hurt that the restaurant gives you free Ice Cream over shaved ice for desert!

Grading Summary

Overall, Bamboo Song 2 receives a 4/5 pho-icons! We'll be getting lost in your neck of the woods in the near future!
