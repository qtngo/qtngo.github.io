To much time on our hands...

Ariadora was built upon the curiosity to poke fun at and push the limits of our imagination.

The year was 1999, two college friends were walking back to their dorms, talking about space and the final frontier and somehow thought, "wouldn't it be cool, if they named a planet, Ariadora?"... well, that word stuck, until recently and the domain was purchased, left idle until... now.

The purpose of Ariadora.com is for Family & Friends to have a safe & fun place to help promote and link their online and offline projects, both virtual and physical. Get some traffic through and also receive comments/advise on improvement, support and growth of new, current and existing projects.

Join #TeamAriadora and let your imagination run wild!
